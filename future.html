Our Future Objectives

In the Short Run

1. Obtain the legal authorization of the Taiwan government.
2. Expand the service region to all parts of Taiwan.
3. The platform can issue or receive orders. It can match orders efficiently, eliminating communication costs and labor costs.
4. Expand stores that can provide services to 80% of the market share.
5. Utilize the media industry to raise public reputation.

In the Long Run

1. Provide a platform for service providers to intervene and develop alliances with local life service providers.
2. Go public and then issue stocks to attract investors and raise funds.
3. Become the leader of this industry and further expand overseas.
4. Establish a complete training mechanism for reserved staff, and carry out special guidance training according to local needs.
5. Establish a systematic franchisee mechanism.
