Our Past

History

March, 2018

Because Natalia forgot to send the package to her parents due to preparing for the exam, it’s not surprising that the idea of helping others deliver the package emerged.

May, 2018

Natalia’s first client, Amber, applied for the package-received services three times in a week.

June, 2018

Amber joined Natalia’s team. They strived to increase reach and somebody to help them.

October, 2018

The third member of the team, Hannah, joined Natalia and Amber. Thanks to Hannah’s ultimate skill on programming, they released their first cellphone APP to help their job, the package-received services.

June, 2019

The APP did it’s great job, however, users and employees were all restricted around NCCU. It seemed challenging to expand the scope of services.

July, 2019

As the specialists in finance and accounting area, Vincent and Michael were invited to the team. So far, the company usher in all of her management teams.

December, 2019

In a normal meeting, Vincent mentioned that he was too busy to buy flowers for his girlfriend. Here came the company’s main concept: Helping everything.

March, 2020

The five rolled out the opinion of Potato Errand as well as new APP. Aiming to run the whole show.

Vision Statement

Agents traditionally give people a negative image of "unprofessional". If the agency can establish a business with integrity, professionalism, enthusiasm, and legal creativity, it will inevitably develop into a new and profitable industry.

We founded Potato Errands to save the time and efforts of running errands for modern busy people, so that everyone can allocate resources to the most suitable place.
